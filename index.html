what we do

Better oracles. Full Control.

High Scale

Our oracles are able to support more than 250 feed updates with a single transaction. Bringing unprecedented high scale data availability to your onchain contracts.

ANY API

With a custom API path walker, you can use the APIs you use already for your data, or use external APIs provided by the best data providers, bringing them onchain.

Low Maintainance

Our oracles have been designed from the ground up for usability and easy maintainance and use. You manage the full data set for your feeds from a simple spreadsheet, have access to a fully automated node to handle the data, and have the granularity to change how often feeds are updated, where they source data and who the signers are.

Key Features

the secret sauce

Choose your signers

You control who provides the data for your oracle. Community members, team, data providers or even Scry, you can choose who can submit your data, how many signers are needed to make the data official and can even change both as your project grows on the fly.

Open Access

You'll have full access to the core source and license to modify as you see fit. Change the node and contracts to suit your needs using a strong and flexible framework. Help make the protocol better and access changes made by others.

Data on the Fly

We allow you to update your oracles with new data on the fly. Simply update your spreadsheet with the new API and information and have the signers restart the node (unless they are on auto mode) and your data will seamlessly start submitting. Change the deployment cycle from days to minutes and iterate at the Crypto pace.
